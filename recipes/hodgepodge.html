Hodgepodge Chili

This is a favorite chili recipe of mine! I got it from my sister and would often enjoy it with her and her husband every time I visited them. Enjoy!

Ingredients:

This recipe calls for the following ingredients:

  • 1 or 2 lbs lean ground beef
  • 3 cans of minestrone soup
  • 2 cans of ranch beans
  • 2 cans of rotel diced tomatoes and green chiles
  • 1 packet of chili seasoning
  • Shredded mexican cheese (to taste)
  • Greek yogurt (to taste)
  • Hot sauce (to taste)

Instructions:

  1. Brown the ground beef in a large pot over medium-high heat. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add chile seasoning to meat Stir in while letting meat cook on low for a few minutes.
  3. Add cans of soup, beans, and rotel to pot. (No need to drain cans.)
  4. Stir contents of pot and let simmer on low for at least 30 minutes. (The longer, the better though!) Take care not to let the meat settle on the bottom and burn.
  5. Add cheese, yogurt, and hot sauce to individual bowls to taste.
  6. Eat lots of chili!
