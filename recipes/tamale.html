Tamale Pie

One of my favorite southwest twists on cornbread. Taken from my friend's grandmother.

Ingredients:

This recipe calls for the following ingredients:

  • 2 lbs of lean ground beef
  • Taco seasoning
  • 2 Tb of tomato paste
  • 1 can of corn
  • 1 can of black beans
  • 1 can of green chiles
  • 2 boxes Jiffy cornbread mix (along with 2/3 cup milk and 2 eggs)
  • 1/4 cup mexican shredded cheese
  • Sour cream

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425ËšF.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a 12-inch cast iron skillet over medium-high heat. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add taco seasoning to meat Stir in while letting meat cook on low for a few minutes.
  4. Stir in tomato paste until it is fully incorporated in the meat
  5. Add cans of corn, black beans, and green chiles to pot. (No need to drain cans.)
  6. Stir contents of skillet and let simmer on low for a few minutes.
  7. While skillet is simmering, stir cornbread ingredients (mix, milk, and eggs) until batter is smooth.
  8. Pour batter carefully over the top of the skillet.
  9. Bake entire skillet, uncovered, in oven for 25 minutes
  10. Remove skillet from oven after it is done baking, and sprinkle cheese on top.
  11. Serve with sour cream to taste.
