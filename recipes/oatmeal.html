Powerball Oatmeal

This recipe is adopted from the powerball recipe at Not Your Average Joe in Oklahoma City. By scaling down the batch ingredients and with the addition of chia seeds, you can make a healthy and filling breakfast oatmeal!

Ingredients:

This recipe calls for the following ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup of old fashioned oats
  • 2 Tb chia seeds
  • 2 Tb flaxseeds
  • 2 Tb shredded sweetened coconut flakes
  • 2 Tb powdered peanut butter (like PB fit)
  • 1 Tb honey
  • Dash of vanilla

Instructions:

  1. Combine oats, chia seeds, flaxseed, coconut, and powdered peanut butter into bowl. Stir to incorporate.
  2. Add enough hot water to fully wet oat mixture.
  3. Let sit two minutes.
  4. Stir in honey and vanilla.
  5. Enjoy!
